Entry No. 010 — Background Notes

My Story

What led me into training, and what I'm building toward.

I began my journey as a trainer with a simple belief: many talented students do not struggle because of lack of intelligence — they struggle because of lack of communication skills, confidence, and career awareness.

Over the last few years, I have worked closely with students from different backgrounds, helping them improve their communication, prepare for interviews, and become more employable. Through classroom training, counseling, and mentoring, I have witnessed how the right guidance can completely change a student's confidence and career direction.

My experience extends beyond training. I have contributed to course creation, student counseling, and program development, including designing two complete training programs from the ground up. These experiences helped me understand not only how students learn, but also what employers and organizations expect from them.

Today, my mission is to help students and young professionals develop the career skills that are rarely taught in traditional education — communication, confidence, interview readiness, workplace effectiveness, and professional growth.

Through training programs, content creation, workshops, and speaking engagements, I aim to make career development practical, relatable, and accessible.

I currently lead training delivery, student guidance, and program development at Shakti Banking Academy, alongside building my independent coaching and content work.
