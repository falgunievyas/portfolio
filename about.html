Background

MY STORY

What led me into training, and what I'm building toward.

Trained 5,000+ students across banking, employability, and communication programs — spanning interview prep, technical upskilling, and workplace readiness.

I began my journey as a trainer with a simple belief: many talented students do not struggle because of lack of intelligence — they struggle because of lack of communication skills, confidence, and career awareness.

Over the last few years, I have worked closely with students from different backgrounds, helping them improve their communication, prepare for interviews, and become more employable.

Through classroom training, counseling, and mentoring, I have witnessed how the right guidance can completely change a student's confidence and career direction.

Today, my mission is to help students and young professionals develop the career skills that are rarely taught in traditional education.

Through training programs, content creation, workshops, and speaking engagements, I aim to make career development practical, relatable, and accessible.
